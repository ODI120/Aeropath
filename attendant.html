Who Are Flight Attendant?

Flight attendants are airline crew members responsible for ensuring the safety, comfort, and well-being of passengers during a flight. They demonstrate safety procedures, assist in emergencies, serve food and drinks, and provide customer service throughout the journey.

Course Breakdown

    In-flight Service
    Emergency Procedures
    First Aid and CPR
    Aviation Security and Grooming

Requirements

    High school certificate
    Pass medical and height/weight standards
    Minimum age: 18–21 (varies by airline)
    Minimum height: 5’2”–5’6” (varies)
    High school certificate
    Fluent in English (additional languages are a plus)
    Excellent grooming and communication skills

Average Duration

    3–6 months

Certifications

    Cabin Crew Attestation (CCA)
    CPR and First Aid Certification

Average Salary (Nigeria)

    Entry level: ₦200,000–₦350,000/month
    Experienced: ₦400,000–₦600,000/month

Job Opportunities

    Commercial airlines
    Business jets
    Charter services
