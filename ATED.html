Who Are Aeronautical Telecomunication Engineer?

Aeronautical Telecommunications Engineers are specialists who design, install, maintain, and manage the communication, navigation, surveillance, and air traffic management (CNS/ATM) systems used in aviation. Their work ensures that aircraft can communicate with air traffic control, navigate safely, and be monitored accurately during flight. They work with systems such as: * Ground-to-air and air-to-ground radio communications * Radar and surveillance systems * Instrument landing systems (ILS) * Satellite navigation and data communication networks. These engineers are essential for the smooth operation of air traffic services, airport infrastructure, and flight safety. They usually work with aviation authorities, airports, or air navigation service providers.
