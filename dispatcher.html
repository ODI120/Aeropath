Who Are Flight Dispatchers?

Flight Dispatchers work with pilots to ensure flight safety by planning routes, checking weather, calculating fuel, and coordinating logistics.

Course Breakdown

    Flight Dispatch Training Course
    Meteorology and Navigation
    Operational Procedures
    Aircraft Performance and Loading

Requirements

    Minimum age: 21
    At least a high school certificate; ND/HND or degree in aviation/related field preferred
    Completion of an NCAA-approved Flight Dispatcher Training Program

Average Duration

    6–12 months

Average Salary (Nigeria)

    Entry level: ₦250,000–₦400,000/month
    Experienced: ₦500,000–₦800,000/month

Job Opportunities

    Airlines
    Charter operators
    Cargo companies
